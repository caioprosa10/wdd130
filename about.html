Welcome to The Rapids! Our mission is to deliver thrilling whitewater adventures while keeping safety and fun at the forefront.

History

Founded in 2010, The Rapids began as a small family-run outfitter. Over the years we’ve guided thousands of adventurers through class III and IV rapids, building a legacy of excitement and trust.

Our guides undergo rigorous training and certification to ensure every trip is both safe and unforgettable. We pride ourselves on our expert team and personalized service.
